Application Guide

The Ritual

Wear it like a secret. Apply it like a ceremony.

01
Preparation

Begin With Clean Skin

Fragrance adheres most powerfully to moisturised, clean skin. Apply an unscented body lotion to pulse points immediately after bathing. Your skin is now a canvas.

02
Application

Target The Pulse Points

Hold the bottle 10–15cm from your pulse points — inside wrists, throat, behind ears. Spray once or twice. Do not rub. Allow the warmth of your body to diffuse the scent naturally.

03
Layering

Build Your Signature

For deeper sillage, apply a second spray to the lower neck. JK fragrances are designed with exceptional longevity — layering creates an unforgettable presence.

04
Storage

Preserve The Obsession

Store away from light and heat. Consistent temperature preserves molecular integrity. The bottle belongs on a shelf in the dark — like the ideas it was born from.

"Apply with intention.
Wear for yourself alone."
